About Our Food

The secret to our food? It’s pretty simple. Source fresh, quality ingredients. Prepare dishes with care and passion. Serve them with a warm smile. In other words, we take pride in what we make and how we share it. Even though we’ve grown a lot over the years, our mission is still the same: Always the best food, made especially for you.

Pizza Dough

100% Canadian Wheat! The secret to our perfectly crispy-chewy crust? Keeping it simple. Our pizza dough is made with 100% Canadian wheat and spring water and crafted by hand for every single order.

Home Style Sauce

You can’t make great pizza without a great sauce. Our traditional home-style sauce is made with fresh ingredients: extra virgin olive oil, sunflower oil, oregano, sea salt, black pepper, garlic and, of course, vine-ripened tomatoes, fresh packed within 6 hours of harvest.

Nutrition & Healthy

Our plant-based protein options have been made available through a strategic partnership with Yves Veggie Cuisine (plant-based pepperoni). Both brands are proven leaders in the plant-based protein space and offer a unique flavour in all their products.
